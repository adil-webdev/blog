Hi, I'm Adil 👋

I'm a software engineering student passionate about technology and continuous learning. My journey began with backend development, where I spent a lot of time learning Ruby on Rails and exploring how modern web applications work behind the scenes. As my interests evolved, I became increasingly drawn to DevOps, cloud infrastructure, automation, and the systems that power software at scale.

This blog is where I document what I learn along the way. You'll find notes, tutorials, project write-ups, and lessons learned about DevOps, backend development, Linux, networking, databases, cloud technologies, and software engineering in general.

I started this blog to organize my thoughts, strengthen my understanding, and share knowledge with others who are on a similar learning journey. The content here reflects my current understanding, and I'll continue updating and improving it as I grow.

When I'm away from the keyboard, you'll usually find me playing video games or exploring new technologies out of curiosity.
